About Me

Growing up...

My name is Anton Sebastian Bilbaeno. My father named me after Anton Chekov, a Russian author of many types of writings. I was born in San Jose, California, but moved to Oregon at the age of four. I have lived here, with the exception of college, ever since.

When we first moved to Oregon we lived in an apartment in Beaverton near where Highway 26 and 217 meet. At that time I was going to Ridgewood Elementary. We lived there for a few years then moved about 10 city blocks away to a house that we rented. We lived there for a few years then moved right next to where our apartment was, to a house that my mother bought.

She was very frugal. I do not know how she did it, but she saved enough for the downpayment on that house, and then while paying the mortgage she saved enough for us both to go to college and not take out ~5% loans from the government. The financial stability aside, my mother is probably one of the biggest factors in me being "successful". And by "successful", I strictly mean obtaining a college degree that enables me to get a good job.
